Design Brief

Get ready to embark on an exciting journey into the heart of the digital wilderness to protect and celebrate endangered species in our local area. We are a passionate group of students committed to making a real difference in the world. We believe that the power of technology, creativity, and collaboration can help us safeguard the incredible creatures that share our planet.

Getting Started:

Let's embark on this adventure to protect our local wildlife. Follow these steps to begin the project:

Step 1: Meet Your Wildlife Friends

  • Learn about the animals and plants in your area through books or online resources.

Step 2: Identify a Challenge

  • Reflect on the challenges these animals face. Are they losing their homes? Do they need better protection? Record your thoughts.

Step 3: Create a Plan

  • Collaborate with your friends and create a plan to address these challenges. Document your ideas in your project journal.

Step 4: Explore Tools

  • Discover the tools you'll use, including cameras and digital resources, to support your project.

Step 5: Collect Data

  • Observe and record your wildlife friends. Take notes, draw pictures, or capture photos.

Step 6: Share Your Discoveries

  • Create a digital report or presentation to showcase your findings.

Step 7: Reflect

  • Reflect on your efforts and their impact on wildlife. Share your thoughts in your project journal.

Necessary Software and Tools:

  • A simple camera or smartphone for photos.
  • Online tools like Google Docs and Google Slides for reports and presentations.

Video Tutorial (if applicable): If you're new to taking photos or using Google Docs and Slides, check out our video tutorial to help you get started.

Remember, you're not alone in this adventure. Don't hesitate to ask your teacher for assistance if you ever feel stuck. Now, go out there and be wildlife protectors!
