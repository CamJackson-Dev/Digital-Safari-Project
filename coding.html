What You'll Learn with Scratch:

  • Sequencing: In the world of Scratch, you'll master the art of putting instructions in the right order. Just like protecting wildlife requires a series of actions, you'll create step-by-step instructions for essential tasks. Imagine writing code to help a virtual animal find its way home!
  • Conditional Statements: Scratch will be your gateway to understanding "if-then" statements. Through interactive games and stories, you'll make decisions based on different conditions. For example, "If the animal is hungry, feed it."
  • Loops: Ever wondered how animations work in your favorite games? You'll dive into loops in Scratch, learning how to create animations and simulations. This might involve creating a loop to mimic a wildlife camera capturing animals in action.
  • Debugging: Just like a wildlife detective, you'll use Scratch to spot and correct errors in your code. Think of it as solving puzzles to make your digital tools work seamlessly.

Teaching Approach with Scratch:

Our teaching approach is designed to be interactive and playful. Scratch's colorful and user-friendly interface makes learning coding an exciting adventure. Guided by our experienced educators, you'll embark on a journey that feels more like creating digital art than coding.

With Scratch, you'll see how technology can be a true superhero, helping us protect our local wildlife and their habitats. Together, we'll use the magic of coding to make a real impact on the environment. Get ready to code with Scratch for conservation!

Showcase Day: A Celebration of Our Journey

Our Digital Safari Project concludes with Showcase Day, where students proudly display their digital tools, data, and solutions. It's a moment of celebration, creativity, and conservation, as our young protectors share their passion for wildlife and the environment. Families, friends, and the school community are all invited to join this special event. Together, we'll applaud the impact our students have made as they present their innovative work. It's a day of pride, learning, and appreciation.
