About our Project

Introduction to protecting endangered animals:

Endangered animals are special creatures on our planet. They need our help because there aren't many of them left. It's like having a few rare treasures that we must keep safe. Why is it so important to protect these animals? Well, they are like pieces of a big puzzle called nature. If we lose these pieces, the puzzle won't be complete, and things can go wrong. When we protect these animals, we're also looking after the places they live and the plants they need. That way, everything stays in balance, just like when you take care of your toys or your favorite books. So, by helping these endangered animals, we are like nature's superheroes, making sure our world stays a wonderful place for everyone, animals, and people. Let's be their heroes!

Who Are We?

We are Year 3 students with big dreams and bigger hearts. We come from diverse backgrounds, but we are united by a common purpose: to be the champions of our local wildlife.

Our Mission:

  • Explore the wild world around us through digital lenses.
  • Discover the stories of endangered animals and their habitats.
  • Create innovative solutions for their survival and flourishing.
  • Share our findings and insights with the world.

What We Do:

  • Observe, research, and protect the animals and plants in our neighbourhood.
  • Develop creative solutions using digital tools and technologies.
  • Learn about sustainability, responsibility, and the vital role we play in conserving our environment.

Join us as we embark on this digital adventure to make a positive impact on our environment. We invite you to explore, learn, and collaborate with us as we become the Digital Safari Explorers!
