Online Safety Matters:

A Digital Safari

As we explore the digital world to protect our local wildlife, it's essential to remember the importance of online safety. Be cautious when sharing personal information and ensure you're interacting with trusted sources. Always ask for permission from a responsible adult before using new online tools or platforms. If you ever come across something that makes you uncomfortable or unsure, immediately let your teacher or a trusted adult know. We're here to ensure your online experience is safe and enjoyable while working on the Digital Safari Project.
